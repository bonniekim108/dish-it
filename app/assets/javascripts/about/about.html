ABOUT

Have you ever had a dish from a restaurant so good that you had to share it with the world?

dish IT lets you share that experience AND lets you see how your dish stacks up against other popular dishes! It's a BATTLE in which you pit your best dish against other great dishes and let the voting determine the last dish standing.

So get in the ring and dish IT!

HOW TO dish IT

Every month, dish IT will pit your dish entry against other entries, and the voting begins.

Users cast their votes weekly for each battle round. At the end of week one, only the top eight dishes (The Great Eight) move on to the next round. At the end of week two, only the top four (The Final 4) advance to week three, in which the final two(Final Round) dishes standing duke it out for Top Dish!
